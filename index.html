Branded QR

Turn any link into a branded QR code

Paste a URL, get the same styled code every time — rounded modules, blue corner markers, and your logo in the middle. Each one is checked to stay scannable before you download.

Waiting for a link…

Tip: print at roughly 2.5 cm / 1 inch wide or larger, keep strong contrast with whatever sits behind it, and test-scan with a couple of phones before a big print run. Very long links make a denser, harder-to-scan code — short, clean URLs work best.
